About Us

It has been a life long dream of ours to run a bed and breakfast. One of our favorite pasttimes as children was playing house. We would create elaborate decorations of our rooms and front door and, in full costume, greet our parents at the front door after work. They would get a tour, be sat down, and served tea. Thirty years later, we took that passion for hosting and serving and made it into a reality. It is truly our pleasure to welcome you to our home and provide the most amazing experience imaginable.
